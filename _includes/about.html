About


I've been baking cakes for family and friends for most of my life. I love to bake with my children, like my mother and I baked together many years ago. As my youngest is about to embark on her first day at Nursery, I've decided to pluck up the courage - and bow to pressure from my friends - and create #prettyniceslice

At the moment I only offer a local pickup or drop off service (depending on your location). I am based near Brockley, Dulwich, Catford, Forest Hill in South east London.
